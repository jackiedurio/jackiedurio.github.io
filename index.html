Mrs. Jackie Durio Hello, Welcome to my page. This is my seventh year at Clements. I have taught for thirty-six years in various districts, and I am certified in English-Language Arts, and Social Studies. I also hold certifications in Gifted Education and English as a Second Language. I am originally from New Mexico and moved to Texas in 2002. While in Texas, I have lived in Denton, Galveston, Seabrook, and now Sugar Land. I have three daughters, four grandchildren, and three dogs. I truly love teaching and the opportunity to help students reach their potential. Please check the other pages for school supplies and summer reading choices. I look forward to working with you and your child.
